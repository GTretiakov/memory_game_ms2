Speed

After you click on "Start" button, the game will randomly show you one number. Memorize the number and click it, you will score 1 point. The game will repeat clicked number and add next random number to the sequence. Every time you repeat the sequence, your score will increase by 1. Be careful! You must repeat the same numbers in the same order to score more points. As soon as you click on a wrong number, you loose. If you feel like the game is too slow or too fast, you can always change game's speed before or even during the game. Improve your memory and attention! Good luck!
